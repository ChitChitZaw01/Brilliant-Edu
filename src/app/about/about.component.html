About Us

Welcome to Brilliant Educational Institution, an educational platform built by passionate volunteers dedicated to supporting the Civil Disobedience Movement (CDM) in Myanmar. We are a collective of educators, activists, and community members, united in our commitment to freedom, justice, and the power of education. As volunteers, we believe that knowledge is a powerful tool for resistance and change. Our goal is to provide accessible learning resources that empower individuals, particularly those affected by the political struggles in Myanmar, to continue their education despite the challenges faced. Through our platform, we aim to support the CDM by helping to keep minds strong, informed, and resilient during these difficult times. We are committed to providing a space for learning, solidarity, and collective action, where every individual can find the tools they need to contribute to a better future for Myanmar. By offering educational content in a variety of subjects, we seek to help rebuild and strengthen the community, one lesson at a time. Join us in our mission to foster hope, resistance, and empowerment through education.
